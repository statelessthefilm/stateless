Director

Manvendra Singh Shekhawat


Manvendra Singh Shekhawat in perusal of his passion towards telling the untold tales went on to study Journalism from Leeds Beckett University and completed his MSc in Journalism in 2015. In 2015 he came back to India and started perusing the stories which were important but often left untouched by mainstream media. He has made documentaries on subjects like suicides in student community, importance of technology in modern warfare, Revenge Porn, Sewage irrigation, modern day influencers and so on.
